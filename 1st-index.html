What if instagram can create a watermark with a function of taking users to the original post when downloading or screenshotting others’ images?


Story Begins

David is a photographer, he used to post his beautiful photos on Twitter, but he notices another account is stealing his work, posts them as if they own it. There are too many accounts that steal image from him. Although he reported to Twitter, but it still can’t change the phenomenon. He is worried about the same thing might be happens again. Then, he asked his friend on Facebook about how to protect his own rights on social media. His friend told him Instagram has a function of forming a watermark when downloading or screenshot the images, and it will take the person to the first poster. So he begins to post his photos on Instagram. After he tried to post on Instagram, he finally realizes that’s true of instagram can create a unique watermark for the first poster. Then, he posts all his experience on Youtube, he wants more people to know it!

Inspiration:It’s a social media world now. Image is one of the most important components for social media. Instagram is a image based platform, users could share their lives there with sharing photos with content, or videos. Because of this, the demand of high quality image are being highly needed for posters. Especially, when people want to get more followers, or likes. The hotter the post is, the more money you can make. In this case, stealing others’ images or videos is a common phenomenon in Instagram.
